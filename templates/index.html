Introducing SPEAKIT

A tool that uses technology to translate sign language, making communication easier for people with hearing or speech impairments.

Features

Speech to Sign Language

Translates spoken words into american sign language (ASL)

SIMAX Animation

SiMAX Animation provides translations for both text and speech recognition.

Speech Recognation

To speak, click or hold the microphone button and start speaking to transcribe your words.

Features

Sign Language to Text

Translates spoken words into american sign language (ASL)

Camera Recognation

Place an ASL hand sign infront of the camera sensor for fingerspelling recognition.

Text Translation

Enter text into the textbox for American Sign Language fingerspelling translation.

Let your hands

Make a sound

SPEAKiT is a fingerspelling translator tool promoting inclusivity between deaf and hearing communities.
